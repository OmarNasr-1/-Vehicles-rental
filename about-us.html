In this website, we have collected for you a group of different vehicles for rent for a period of time that you specify yourself. At first, you can browse and see all the vehicles suitable for rent, but if you want to rent, you must follow some steps, which are:
  • Create a new account.
  • Confirm your account through the account given on registration.
  • Log in and go to the rental page.
  • Choose the appropriate vehicle and then book it.

If you own a company that rents a type of vehicle, such as cars, and you want to offer your service on our website, you can follow these steps to create an owner account for you.
  • Create a new user account from registration page.
  • Confirm your account through the account given on registration.
  • After activating your account from the mail, contact us through the contact page at the end of the home page.
  • Then we make your account an owner account and you can register with it through the admin button at the top of the home page in the Nav bar.

After completing your application to become an owner, you can register for the vehicle management page, and then you will find some important terms such as :
  • Orders : This page contains all the rental requests made by users on your vehicles on the site
  • manage | cars :On this page, you can add a new vehicle to the site with its full details and also view all your vehicles that you have added to the site and the possibility of modification to it
